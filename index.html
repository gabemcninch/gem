The roads today were pretty bad compared to the roads the day before. While driving today I realized that this bad weather has given me the chance to utilize the true unpowerful powers of the mighty Toyota Prius. Since the driving conditions were shit, I began to drive at the speed a Prius was designed for, 45 miles per hour. After continuing to mock this persona of the typical Prius driver, my fuel mileage increased, making up for the fuel spent trying to get out of my drive way. This is the first blog post for a blog that I plan on doing until I find it usless.
